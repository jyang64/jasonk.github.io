Learn

Discover the basics of the game.

What you need:
  • Racket
  • Ball
  • Court
  • At least 1 friend
How hit a ball:
  1. Get in position! Use lots of little steps instead of a few big ones.
  2. Pivot your body sideways, so your body is perpindicular to the net, and you are an arms length away from where the ball is going to go.
  3. Swing from low to high, following through over your shoulder.
